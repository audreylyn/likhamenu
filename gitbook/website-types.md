# Website Types & Templates

This document outlines different website types and their recommended section configurations for optimal user experience and conversion.

## 1. The "Service / Agency" Site
**Best for:** Consulting firms, marketing agencies, cleaning services, or your own freelance portfolio.

**Goal:** To explain what you do and prove you are good at it.

**The Recipe:**
- ✅ Hero Section: (Headline: "We Build Digital Solutions")
- ✅ Benefits Section: (Use this for "Our Services" - e.g., Web Design, SEO)
- ✅ Team Section: (Show the experts behind the work)
- ✅ Testimonials Section: (Client reviews)
- ✅ Contact Section: (Lead generation form)

## 2. The "SaaS / App" Landing Page
**Best for:** Someone selling a software tool, a mobile app, or a digital course.

**Goal:** To handle objections and get a subscription.

**The Recipe:**
- ✅ Hero Section: (Show the app screen)
- ✅ Benefits Section: (Key features of the software)
- ✅ Pricing Section: (Free vs. Pro tier)
- ✅ FAQ Section: (Answer questions like "Can I cancel anytime?")
- ✅ CallToAction Section: (Big button: "Start Free Trial")

## 3. The "Creative Portfolio"
**Best for:** Photographers, Architects, Interior Designers, or Makeup Artists.

**Goal:** Visual impact. Less reading, more looking.

**The Recipe:**
- ✅ Hero Section: (Full-screen background image)
- ✅ Gallery Section: (The main focus—a grid of their best work)
- ✅ About Section: (Artist biography)
- ✅ Contact Section: (Booking inquiries)
- ❌ Skip: Benefits or Pricing (Keep it minimal and custom)

## 4. The "Event / Conference" Page
**Best for:** Webinars, workshops, local meetups, or concerts.

**Goal:** Get ticket sales or registrations.

**The Recipe:**
- ✅ Hero Section: (Event Title, Date, Time)
- ✅ About Section: (What is the event about?)
- ✅ Team Section: (Use this for "Speakers" or "Performers")
- ✅ Pricing Section: (Ticket tiers: Early Bird, VIP)
- ✅ FAQ Section: (Venue details, parking, refund policy)
- ✅ CallToAction Section: ("Register Now" sticky button)

## 5. The "Restaurant / Food" Site (Non-Ordering)
**Best for:** Fine dining or sit-down cafes that care more about vibes than online delivery.

**Goal:** Get people to book a table or visit.

**The Recipe:**
- ✅ Hero Section: (Video background of food being cooked)
- ✅ Featured Section: (Chef's Specials of the month)
- ✅ Gallery Section: (Photos of the interior/ambiance)
- ✅ Testimonials Section: (Food reviews)
- ✅ Contact Section: (Map location and Reservation phone number)

## 6. The "E-commerce / Online Store"
**Best for:** Selling physical products, handmade goods, or retail businesses.

**Goal:** Drive sales and build customer loyalty.

**The Recipe:**
- ✅ Hero Section: (Showcase featured products or seasonal offers)
- ✅ Products Section: (Product catalog with categories)
- ✅ Benefits Section: (Why shop here - free shipping, quality guarantee)
- ✅ Testimonials Section: (Customer reviews)
- ✅ Contact Section: (Customer service and returns info)

## 7. The "Blog / Personal Website"
**Best for:** Writers, bloggers, journalists, or personal branding.

**Goal:** Build audience, share knowledge, and establish expertise.

**The Recipe:**
- ✅ Hero Section: (Author introduction and latest post)
- ✅ Featured Section: (Pinned or popular posts)
- ✅ About Section: (Author biography and credentials)
- ✅ Testimonials Section: (Reader testimonials or press mentions)
- ✅ Contact Section: (Newsletter signup and contact form)
